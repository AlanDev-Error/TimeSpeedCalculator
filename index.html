The Equation

Time = UniverseSpeed × MassOfUniverse × State + c / Void
State = (ObjectMass ÷ ∞) + 1

Principles

Based on the ideas of time dilation, mass and gravity affect how time flows. In vacuum, time runs at its fastest form (1 s^s) or 1...e+55 billion s^s. Near extreme gravity, such as black holes, time slows down and may even reach a forbidden NULL state.

Values

  • Universe scaling factor: 3.09 × 10¹⁸ × 700
  • Mass of Universe: ~1.5 × 10⁵³ kg
  • Speed of light (c): 299,792,458 m/s
